# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

**1. The Zero-PII Privacy Commitment**
We operate on a foundational "Zero-PII" (Zero Personally Identifiable Information) architecture. Unlike traditional real estate, home services, or lead-generation websites, this platform does not utilize lead-capture forms. We do not collect, process, store, or sell personal contact information. You are never required to submit your name, email address, physical mailing address, or telephone number to access the algorithmic cost estimates and analytical data provided on our site.

**2. Independence from Lead Brokering and Anonymity of Communication**
Because we do not collect personal contact information, we cannot and do not sell consumer profiles to third-party contractors, aggressive marketing agencies, or data brokers. Your interactions with the data on this platform remain entirely anonymous. When you choose to initiate a phone call via the toll-free numbers provided on our site, the telephonic interaction occurs directly between you and the third-party structural engineering or contracting network. We do not intercept, record, transcribe, or store the personal details, audio, or caller identification metadata of these telephone communications.

**3. Automated Web Analytics and Non-Identifying Data Aggregation**
While we strictly enforce a Zero-PII policy regarding your personal identity, maintaining a highly functional digital platform requires the use of standard, anonymized tracking technologies to monitor website performance and optimize our systems. We employ third-party analytics services, which utilize cookies and similar tracking protocols to collect non-identifying telemetry. This includes aggregated data regarding browser types, generalized geographic regions (such as the zip code-level analysis required to query our algorithmic soil databases), device specifications, referral sources, and page interaction metrics.

**4. Third-Party Vendor Compliance and User Control**
All analytical data collection is conducted in strict compliance with our third-party vendors' terms of service, ensuring proactive measures are taken to prevent the transmission of any PII to our analytics providers. Users possess the fundamental right to manage, block, or disable tracking cookies through their individual web browser settings or via available opt-out browser extensions. By utilizing this platform, you acknowledge the use of these anonymized performance-tracking mechanisms as outlined in this policy.
